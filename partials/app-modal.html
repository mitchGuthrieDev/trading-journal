Overview

Load data

Pick a CSV exported from your trading platform — Blotterbook detects the format, then hit Import to merge it in. Re-uploads merge by a stable trade id, so importing an overlapping export is always safe. The platform choice applies to this upload only.

Backup & restore

A backup is a single JSON file holding your trades, day-notes, and setup. Restoring merges it back in — existing trades are de-duplicated, so re-importing is always safe. Keep a backup before clearing data or switching browsers.

Edit a trade to add tags, a note, and screenshots — tags also power the Tag filter. Deleting a trade can't be undone (re-load the CSV or a backup to restore it).

Saved filters

Apply, rename, or delete the filter views you saved from the filter bar (Save filter).

Danger zone

Wipes every trade, day-note, and saved setup from this browser. Export a backup first if you might want it back.

The Plan

Roadmap

Where Blotterbook is headed — the big picture, not a task list. You already get a full futures journal with a true-cost & tax dashboard; next come numbers you can trust to the cent, deeper journaling, and sync across your devices — all built privacy-first. For the per-release detail, see the Changelog.

Available now

  • ✓
    Import from your platform in seconds

    Drop in a CSV from TradingView, Tradovate, Rithmic, Sierra Chart, TradeStation, MotiveWave, Webull, IBKR or Schwab/thinkorswim — the format is auto-detected, and re-uploads merge so only new trades are added.

  • ✓
    Your true P&L, not just gross

    Every trade is costed with broker commissions, exchange & NFA fees, your data-feed and platform subscriptions, and a Section 1256 tax estimate — so you see net and real take-home, plus the break-even you need per trade.

  • ✓
    A dashboard that fills the screen

    An equity curve with gross / net / take-home overlays, a P&L calendar, win rate, profit factor, drawdown and more — edge-to-edge on a wide monitor, single column on your phone.

  • ✓
    Arrange it your way

    Drag to reorder the modules, collapse what you don't need, and save named workspace layouts to switch between views.

  • ✓
    Filter, focus, and journal

    Slice by date range, symbol, side, session or weekday and save the filter views you reuse; keep day-by-day notes plus per-trade tags, notes, and screenshots.

  • ✓
    Reports worth sharing

    A clean, condensed performance report you can preview and download as PDF, image, or Markdown — or email to yourself.

  • ✓
    Private by default

    Everything runs and stays in your browser — no account, nothing uploaded. Back up or restore your entire journal as a single file anytime, and try it first on the live sample-data demo.

In progress

  • ●
    Numbers you can trust to the centHigh priority

    Today's fees and tax rates are careful estimates. We're wiring them to official sources — CME/exchange fee schedules, per-broker data-feed price lists, and per-state tax tables, refreshed on a schedule — so your cost and take-home figures are exact, not approximate.

  • ●
    Broader, verified platform coverage

    Confirming each importer against real exports, widening the contract/point-value coverage, and adding a manual column-mapping fallback for any format we don't yet recognize.

  • ●
    Deeper journaling

    The analytics serious traders expect: R-multiple and risk tracking, MAE/MFE (how far each trade ran for and against you), and setup tagging — toward full journal parity.

Planned

  • □
    Sync across your devices$5/mo

    End-to-end-encrypted sync of your trades, notes, tags, and saved views, so your journal follows you to any device — and we never see your data. The one optional paid add-on; everything else stays free.

  • □
    Connect your broker directly

    Optionally pull fills, commissions, and live rates straight from your platform — and skip the CSV step entirely.

  • □
    Replay any trade on a chartStretch

    Reconstruct a price chart with your entry and exit for each trade, straight from the imported data, for visual review.

  • □
    A power-user workspace

    Resizable, snapping modules; multiple tabbed workspaces; and Portfolios that capture a full set of layouts and data — a trading-terminal feel for traders who want it.
